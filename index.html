Story
The journey

Toufic started training at 13 after being overweight and bullied. Sport became the first place where he rebuilt discipline, confidence, and identity. Since then, he has raced regularly in Lebanon, completed two marathons, moved alone to France, co-built Sira into a 25-person operation — all at the same time.

2019

The transformation

Overweight and bullied at 13. One night, one decision. Full body transformation in 6 months — no coach, no team, just discipline.

2023

First races, first wins

Won ISF 21KM Half Marathon on debut at 16. Raced almost every weekend in Lebanon. Co-founded Sira.

2024

Arena without armor

Ran 10K with broken leg — featured on MTV Lebanon. Moved alone to France with 1 day's notice. First marathon: Beirut, double podium.

2025

Fire year

Registered Sira SARL. Sub-1:30 half marathon. Managing 25 people while studying and training. Hospitals, exams, heartbreak — still showing up.

2026

Warsaw — 7 days before turning 20

Prague Marathon PB 3:42:37 · Montpellier HM PB 1:29:26 · IRONMAN 70.3 Warsaw June 7. 113 km. First triathlon.
