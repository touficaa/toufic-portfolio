Story
The journey

Toufic grew up between two homes after his parents divorced at birth. At 13, overweight and bullied, he started training and working on himself. Sport became the first place where he learned discipline. Business became the second.

Age 13 — 2019

The start

Parents divorced at birth. Grew up between two homes. At 13, overweight and bullied — started training and working at the same time. Tried to become an adult earlier than normal. First business attempt. Failed. Tried again.

2019 – 2022

Entrepreneur before athlete

Started his first business at 13. Failed 7 times across different projects. At 17, while still in high school, hired by a US video production company as a lead producer. Built Sira into a 25+ person company before turning 18.

2023

First races, first wins

Started running in 2021. Started tracking seriously in 2022. By 2023, racing almost every weekend in Lebanon. Won ISF 21KM Half Marathon on debut. Ran Beirut Marathon 8.5K with broken leg — featured on MTV Lebanon. Co-founded Sira before turning 18.

2024

Built while moving

Continued racing across Lebanon. Multiple podiums including 1st U20 finishes at Kharbe, Qabr Shmoon, Tripoli (featured on North Lebanon Sports), and Yanta. Moved alone to France.

2025

Fire year

Exams, Sira, cash pressure, training, and responsibility at the same time. First marathon: OMT Beirut 3:48:22 (2nd U20, 1st 542 Program). Registered Sira SARL. Built the team to 25 people.

2026

First IRONMAN 70.3. Seven days before 20.

Prague Marathon PB 3:42:37 · Montpellier HM PB 1:29:26 · IRONMAN 70.3 Warsaw June 7. 113 km.
