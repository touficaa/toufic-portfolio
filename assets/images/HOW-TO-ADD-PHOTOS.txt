====================================================
TOUFIC PORTFOLIO - HOW TO ADD PHOTOS
====================================================

This folder structure is built to match the website design.
Drop your photos into the matching folders below.

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: hero/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Hero portrait shots (top of Home and Story pages)
RECOMMENDED: 2-3 vertical photos, high quality
NAMING:
  - hero-main.jpg      Main portrait (used on Home)
  - hero-story.jpg     Story page portrait (more intimate)
  - hero-action.jpg    Action shot (optional)

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: races/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Race-specific photos for race cards
RECOMMENDED: 6-10 photos
NAMING (match race names):
  - beirut-marathon.jpg
  - prague-marathon.jpg
  - montpellier.jpg
  - envolez-vous.jpg
  - allure-bleue.jpg
  - ironman-warsaw.jpg

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: gallery/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: General gallery photos (Story page)
RECOMMENDED: 10-20 of your best shots
NAMING: 1.jpg, 2.jpg, 3.jpg, etc.

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: podiums/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Podium and medal photos
NAMING:
  - podium-isf-2023.jpg
  - podium-beirut-2025.jpg
  - podium-barja-2025.jpg
  - podium-envolez-2026.jpg
  - podium-allure-2026.jpg

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: sira/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Sira company photos (Story page)
RECOMMENDED: 3-6 photos
NAMING:
  - sira-team.jpg      Team photo
  - sira-workshop.jpg  Workshop photo
  - sira-event.jpg     Event photo

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: training/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Training, long runs, gym photos
NAMING:
  - training-1.jpg, training-2.jpg, etc.

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: travel/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Travel photos (Lebanon, France, mother trip)
NAMING:
  - travel-lebanon.jpg
  - travel-france.jpg
  - travel-geneva.jpg

----------------------------------------------------
FOLDER: ../logos/
----------------------------------------------------
PURPOSE: Brand/partner logos
RECOMMENDED FORMAT: PNG with transparent background
NAMING:
  - sira.png
  - ray.png
  - mediascaling.png
  - heracles.png
  - roseup.png
  - leklub.png

====================================================
IMPORTANT NOTES
====================================================

1. Use lowercase for all filenames
2. Use hyphens instead of spaces (race-name.jpg, not "Race Name.jpg")
3. JPG format for photos, PNG for logos
4. Max 2MB per photo (compress at tinyjpg.com if needed)
5. Vertical photos work best for hero shots
6. Horizontal photos work best for gallery and race cards

After adding photos, refresh your browser to see them appear.
The website is designed to gracefully show placeholders for any
missing photos, so you can add them in batches at your own pace.
