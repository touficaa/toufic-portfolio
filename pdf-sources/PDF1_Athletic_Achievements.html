Athlete profile
The athlete.
From overweight and bullied at thirteen, to a competitive endurance athlete at nineteen — built one race at a time.

Toufic Abou Ali is a Lebanese endurance athlete based in Montpellier, France. He competes in road racing and triathlon, with podium results across Lebanon and France between 2023 and 2026.

He ran his first half marathon at sixteen and won. His first marathon at eighteen resulted in a double podium at Beirut Marathon 2025. By nineteen he had set personal bests of sub-1:30 for the half marathon and 3:42:37 for the full marathon.

He will line up for his first IRONMAN 70.3 in Warsaw on June 7, 2026 — seven days before turning twenty.
