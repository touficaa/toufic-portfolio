Identity
Who I am.
I am not a professional athlete. I am a student, an entrepreneur, and a human who refuses to lose to himself.
- Toufic Abou Ali

I am a Lebanese endurance athlete born on 14 June 2006 in Beirut. I currently live in Montpellier, France, where I balance competitive road racing and triathlon with university studies and building my company.

Endurance sport is not a side activity for me. It is the discipline that holds the rest of my life together. Every long run, every race, every early morning at the pool is a deliberate act of building a version of myself that can carry pressure without breaking.

I train alone, plan my own programs, and race without a sponsor or a coach. My results come from method, not from access.

I read more than 200 books through my teenage years and routinely work 16 to 18 hour days across training, business, and study during heavy periods. Volume and consistency are my edge.

Origin
The transformation.
2019
Age 13
The night I decided I would not stay the way I was.
Overweight, bullied, insecure - and one decision later, everything started.

At thirteen years old I was overweight, insecure, angry, and being bullied. I was angry at my body, at my circumstances, and at the version of myself I had become. There was no coach in my life, no team, no athletic environment to push me. I had nothing except a mirror and a decision.

One night I made the decision that I would not stay the way I was. I started training that same night. I changed how I ate, how I moved, and how I spent my time. For six months I trained in silence and isolation - no posting, no validation, no shortcuts.

By the end of those six months, I had completed a full physical transformation. More importantly, I had built the discipline that has carried every part of my life since. The body changed. The identity changed. The standard I hold myself to was set that year, and it has never moved.

"I started training the same night I decided I would not stay the way I was. The body followed because the decision was final."

Evolution - The mental journey, not just the races
Five eras.
01
2019 - 2020
Discipline born from pain
The transformation year and the year after. No external structure, no validation, no team. Discipline forged in silence. Through COVID lockdown I held the training when no one was watching. The foundation everything else is built on.
02
2021 - 2022
Split identity
Gym and growth coexisted with distractions I could no longer enjoy without guilt. The internal war between who I was becoming and what I was still holding onto. First training sessions logged on Strava in October 2022.
03
2023
Chaos and repetition
The year that trained my tolerance for pressure. Almost every weekend racing in Lebanon. Won my first half marathon at sixteen. Lived through the heaviest period of Lebanese instability while still showing up to start lines.
04
2024
Entering the arena
Ran a 10K with a broken leg and finished. Featured on MTV Lebanon. Hired as lead producer at Media Scaling, a US video production company. Moved alone to France with one day's notice before my flight.
05
2025 - 2026
Systems over ego
Fire year. First marathon - double podium in Beirut. Founded Sira SARL. Heartbreak. Hospital. Financial pressure. Co-founded the RAY association. Through all of it - Prague Marathon PB, three French podiums, four personal bests, and IRONMAN 70.3 Warsaw on the line.

Selected moments of pressure
Built through pressure.

Every athlete has results. Fewer have a record of how they performed when things broke. These are the moments that shaped my tolerance for pressure.

01
2024 - Age 17
The broken leg 10K
Lined up for a 10K race with a broken leg. Finished the race. The story was picked up by MTV Lebanon, one of the country's leading television channels, as a feature on resilience.
02
2024 - Age 18
Migration with one day's notice
Decided to move from Lebanon to France the night university applications closed. Secured my visa fast and finalised it one day before my flight. Moved alone to a new country and built a life from zero.
03
August 2025 - Age 19
Mother's visit and goodbye
My mother travelled with me through France, Switzerland, and Italy in 2025. One night in Geneva we slept outside in a park and she covered me when I was cold. I took her to Marseille airport on 22 August and was alone in France again.
04
Late 2025 - Age 19
Two emergency hospital visits
Went to the emergency hospital twice in late 2025 - once for inflammation, once for a reaction to antibiotics. Kept working, training, and building Sira through the entire period. Did not miss key sessions or deadlines.
05
22 Dec 2025 - Age 19
Heartbreak
Experienced one of the most painful emotional moments of my life. Two days of crying. Then back to training, gym, studying, and building. Turned the pain into focus and ran the 10K personal best of 39:42 forty days later.
06
3 May 2026 - Age 19
Prague Marathon - racing injured
Started Prague Marathon with an injured left leg. Held top-600 pace through 5K. Refused to drop out as the injury impacted my second half. Finished in 3:42:37 - a marathon PB. Five weeks before IRONMAN 70.3 Warsaw.
