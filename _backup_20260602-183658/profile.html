01
- Identity

Who I am.

I am Toufic Abou Ali, a Lebanese student entrepreneur based in France, Founder & CEO of Sira, endurance athlete, and creator. My story is built around one theme: transforming pressure into proof.

Born on June 14, 2006, I am currently nineteen and preparing to complete IRONMAN 70.3 Warsaw on June 7, 2026, one week before turning twenty.

I live between Lebanon and France. I compete in road racing and triathlon while building a company and completing my studies. I do not have a coach, a sponsor, or a club. What I have is a system, a story, and a refusal to lose to myself.

02
- Origin

The beginning.

2006
My parents divorced at birth. I grew up living between my mother and my father.
2013-19
At thirteen I was overweight, insecure, angry. I was being bullied.
2019
One night I decided I was not staying like this. I started training the same day.
I did my first big transformation in about six months - with diet, training, isolation, and silence.

There was no coach, no team, no athletic environment to push me. I had nothing except a mirror and a decision. The body changed in six months. The discipline that has carried every part of my life since was set that year, and it has never moved.

03
- Evolution

Five eras.

The mental journey - not just the races.

2020
Discipline through isolation
I kept discipline even with no proof and no validation. COVID lockdown. Training alone with no one watching, no race calendar, no audience. The year that taught me that consistency does not require permission.
2021
Split identity
I lived a split identity - gym and growth, but also games and distractions. I could not enjoy distraction anymore without guilt. The internal war between who I was becoming and what I was still holding onto.
End 2022
Meeting Zayn - purpose begins
After meeting Zayn I started thinking seriously about purpose and what business actually fits me. The first serious shift from doing things to choosing things.
2023
Chaos and repetition
The year that trained my tolerance for pressure. Almost every weekend racing in Lebanon. Won my first half marathon at sixteen. Lived through the heaviest period of Lebanese instability while still showing up to start lines.
2024
Entering the arena without armor
Ran a 10K with a broken leg and finished. Featured on MTV Lebanon. Hired as lead producer at Media Scaling. Moved alone to France with one day's notice before my flight.
2025 - 2026
Fire year - systems over ego
Survival mode, heartbreak, exams, hospital, financial pressure - still showing up. First marathon, double podium in Beirut. Founded Sira SARL. Early 2026: carrying business, university, training, and responsibilities at the same time.

I decided to move to France the night university applications were closing. I had two choices: drop out, or go to France. I took the risk. I secured my visa fast and finalised it one day before my flight. I moved alone to a new country with no real plan.

06
- Business journey

Seven failures before the answer.

I failed in business more than seven times since I was thirteen. I tried many different business models. I worked day and night for more than a year trying to build something successful. I made zero money.

✕
Multiple ecommerce brands - all failed
✕
Party and event-type projects - all got cancelled
✕
Fitness program with athletes partnership - stopped early
✕
A crushing loss that ended in thousands of dollars in debt
- The realisation

I kept choosing the wrong business for me every time. I was not failing because I did not work hard - I was working hard on the wrong thing.

After meeting Zayn in late 2022 and through 2023 I was deep on figuring out purpose and business fit. I spent weeks researching the most successful businesses and business owners in the world. I found patterns and built a formula in my head for choosing purpose and the right business or career. I wanted to prove myself while still failing and experimenting.

Lebanon's war and crisis made everything heavier and pushed urgency. End of 2023, that pressure and the environment pushed me toward building something real and aligned.

Built through pressure.

01
2024 · Age 17

Lined up for a 10K race with a broken leg. Finished the race. The story was picked up by MTV Lebanon, one of the country's leading television channels, as a feature on resilience.

02
2024 · Age 18

Migration with one day's notice

Decided to move from Lebanon to France the night university applications closed. Secured my visa fast and finalised it one day before my flight. Moved alone to a new country and built a life from zero.

03
Summer 2025 · Age 19

Surprise return to Lebanon

I surprised people in Lebanon by coming back alone without telling anyone. A return that felt like reclaiming the place that had shaped me.

04
August 2025 · Age 19

Mother's France trip and Geneva park

My mother travelled with me to France in 2025 and she took care of everything. We travelled Toulouse, Switzerland, Geneva, Interlaken, Lauterbrunnen, and the Italy side. One night in Geneva we slept outside in a park and she kept her arm on me and covered me when I was cold. I took her to the airport in Marseille on August 22 and I was alone again.

05
Late 2025 · Age 19

Two emergency hospital visits

Went to the emergency hospital twice in late 2025 - once for inflammation, once for a reaction to antibiotics. I still kept working, training, and building during that period. I survived exam periods on very little sleep and still showed up.

06
December 22, 2025 · Age 19

Heartbreak

I fell deeply in love in 2024-2025 and it was intense and real. I crossed my own boundaries even when I knew the ending might be sad. My heart got broken on December 22, 2025. My friends were with me right after - Majed and Sari came and slept over and listened. I cried for the first time in a long time, two days of crying. After that I still kept running, gym, and building, and I turned the pain into focus.

07
December 2025

A neutral and dark holiday season

Christmas and New Year felt neutral and dark that year. The continuing weight of what happened underneath everything else.

08
January 1, 2026

New Year at Nicole's house

I spent New Year at Nicole's with Melanie, Sari, Majed, Mathieu, Nicole, and David. We stayed until 2 AM. Friends slept at my house after. The house felt alive but the pain was still there under it.

09
May 3, 2026 · Age 19

Prague Marathon - racing injured

Started Prague Marathon with an injured left leg. Held top-600 pace through 5K. Refused to drop out as the injury impacted my second half. Finished in 3:42:37 - a marathon personal best. Five weeks before IRONMAN 70.3 Warsaw.

The manifesto.

- Why I am submitting to the Lebanese Sports Federation

I want to be officially registered as a Lebanese athlete so that every result I post abroad - in France, in the Czech Republic, in Poland, and beyond - is counted under the Lebanese flag. I have built every kilometre of this career independently. Federation recognition is the structural step that allows my work to represent something larger than myself.
