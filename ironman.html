Why This Matters
The story behind the race

More than a race.

A 19-year-old Lebanese founder and endurance athlete based in France — attempting IRONMAN 70.3 before turning 20 while managing a 25-person company, completing university exams, and training across three disciplines. Self-coached and self-funded so far. Still showed up without perfect timing and conditions.

Lebanese Youth

Representing a generation that builds and competes under pressure — wars, financial crisis, personal hardship. Still showing up.

Diaspora Story

Born in Lebanon. Based in France. Racing in Poland. The Lebanese flag carried across borders — origin does not limit ambition.

Pressure into Proof

Business, university, training — all at once, all at 19. Warsaw is not a goal. It is a result of showing up every day.

"I had many reasons to quit. I chose one reason not to."

— Toufic Abou Ali · May 2026
